Overview

Project Overview

This project involved designing a complete personal brand identity system from the ground up. The goal was to create a visual language that communicates professionalism, creativity, and a UX-first mindset across all touchpoints.

Every element — from the logo mark to the color palette to the typography — was chosen with intention, ensuring the brand feels cohesive whether it appears on a business card, a website, or a social media banner.

Challenge & Response
The Problem

What needed solving?

Without a cohesive brand identity, a designer's work can feel disconnected — great individually but lacking the consistency that builds trust and recognition with potential clients or employers.

The Solution

How I solved it

Developed a unified identity system with a custom logo, defined color palette, and typographic guidelines that work across digital and print formats — all grounded in UX principles of clarity and intent.
