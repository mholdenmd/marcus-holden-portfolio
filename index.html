About Me

Design With a Purpose

"Designing Experiences That Actually Work."

I'm Marcus Holden — a creative who believes design is only as good as the experience it creates. I don't just make things look good; I make them work better.

From brand identities to digital art, every decision I make starts with one question: does this actually serve the person using it? That mindset drives everything — the layouts, the color choices, the typography, all of it.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me studying what makes everyday experiences feel effortless — because the best design is the kind people never have to think about.
